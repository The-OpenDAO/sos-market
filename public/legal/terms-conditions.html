  1. “Products and/or Services” includes but is not limited to the “SOS Market” and OpenDAO SOS website and “WEB3 Protocol”.
  2. “SOS Market” is a decentralized and autonomous protocol for prediction markets built on the “Ethereum Blockchain”.
    1. The SOS Market has been built by the OpenDAO community to create markets for the purpose of trading the outcome of events by which users are able to create a prediction market and other users may place trades on the outcomes of the prediction market based on external data (such users referred to as “Participants”).
    2. Trusted entities (“Oracles”) provide data feeds that determine the outcome of the prediction market.
    3. Participants are paid out by the “Smart Contract” based on the success of their prediction.
  3. “WEB3 Protocol” in the context of the Ethereum Blockchain refers to decentralized applications (“Dapps”) that run on the Ethereum “Blockchain”.
  4. “Ethereum Blockchain” is a decentralized, open-source blockchain with Smart Contract functionality. Ether is the native cryptocurrency of the platform.
  5. “Oracles” are data feeds that connect the Ethereum Blockchain to off-chain, real-world information. For example, prediction market Dapps use oracles to settle payments based on events.
  6. “Smart Contract” is a program that runs on the Ethereum Blockchain. It's a collection of code (its functions) and data (its state) that resides at a specific address on the Ethereum Blockchain.
  7. “Blockchain” a public database that is updated and shared across many computers in a network.
